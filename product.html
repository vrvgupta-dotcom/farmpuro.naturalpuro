The Recipe

The colour of early monsoon.

"We grind it the same morning the coriander arrives. Any later and the green starts to dull — and you can taste it." — Asha, our head cook

Made from coriander and mint picked at peak freshness, hand-ground on a granite sil-batta the same day so nothing goes bitter or grey. A small fistful of green chilli, a pinch of rock salt, a squeeze of lime — that's it. Nothing else. Nothing artificial.

Works as a cooling counterpoint to anything rich or spiced. We've watched it disappear faster than the kebabs at family dinners, and it's the only chutney that genuinely doubles as a sandwich spread without anyone complaining.
